The Challenge

How should a worship education platform grow to become indispensable for church teams while maintaining its unique spiritual formation positioning?

📱
Mobile Gap
60% of worship activity happens mobile (backstage, soundcheck), but current platform is desktop-centric. Losing engagement during high-intent moments.
Critical Priority
👥
Collaboration Friction
1,480 multi-seat accounts can't coordinate within the platform. Teams forced to use email/Slack/text for what they learned on Psallo.
High Impact
🎯
Search Overwhelm
750+ songs with no intelligent recommendations or personalized onboarding. New users drop off after signup.
Quick Win
🏢
Enterprise Readiness
80 large accounts (11+ seats) lack admin dashboards, SSO, and integrations. Leaving revenue on table.
High ACV

Roadmap Overview

Three phases balancing quick wins with strategic bets over 24 months

Phase 1
Foundation & Quick Wins
Months 0-6
  • ✅ Mobile-responsive web experience
  • ✅ Team planning & setlist builder
  • ✅ Analytics foundation
  • ✅ Onboarding personalization
  • ✅ Content search improvements
Phase 2
Growth & Differentiation
Months 6-12
  • 📱 Native mobile apps (iOS & Android)
  • 🎼 Rehearsal & practice tools
  • 🔗 Integration platform (Planning Center)
  • 📊 Admin dashboard & team management
  • 🤖 AI-powered recommendations
Phase 3
Scale & Innovation
Months 12-24
  • 🎨 Creator marketplace & platform
  • 🏢 Enterprise suite & white-label
  • 👨‍🎓 Youth worship expansion
  • 🎭 Worship arts (production, visuals)
  • 🌐 Platform ecosystem
